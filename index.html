What is Paddle Tennis?

Paddle tennis is a fun, fast-paced sport that combines elements of tennis and squash. Played on a smaller court with solid paddles and a perforated ball, it’s easy to learn and perfect for all ages.

Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced player, paddle tennis offers a unique blend of strategy, skill, and social interaction. Join the paddle tennis community today!
